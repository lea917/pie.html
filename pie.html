Grandma's lemons Meringue Pie

A spin of the standard

serves 8 people

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all purpode flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons,juiced and zested
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1(9 inch)pie crust ,baked
  • 4 eggs whites
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar
